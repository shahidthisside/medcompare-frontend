Frequently Asked Questions

How does MedCompare work?

MedCompare searches multiple online pharmacies to find the best prices for your medications. Simply enter the name of your medicine in the search box, and we'll show you price comparisons from various trusted sources.

Is MedCompare free to use?

Yes, MedCompare is completely free for all users. We don't charge any fees for our price comparison service.

How accurate are the prices shown?

We strive to provide the most up-to-date pricing information. Our system regularly checks prices from partner pharmacies, but we recommend verifying the final price on the pharmacy's website before making a purchase.

Can I purchase medicines directly through MedCompare?

No, MedCompare is a price comparison service only. When you find a medicine at a price you like, we'll direct you to the pharmacy's website to complete your purchase.

Do I need to create an account to use MedCompare?

No, you can use our basic search features without creating an account. However, creating a free account allows you to save your favorite medicines and receive price alerts.

Which pharmacies do you compare?

We compare prices from major online pharmacies and medication discount programs. Our network includes most major pharmacy chains and trusted online medication retailers.

Can I compare prices for prescription medications?

Yes, MedCompare shows prices for both prescription and over-the-counter medications. However, you'll still need a valid prescription to purchase prescription medications from the pharmacies.
